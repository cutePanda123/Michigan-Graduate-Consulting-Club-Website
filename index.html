Mission Statement:

The Michigan Graduate Consulting Club (MGCC) aims to support University of Michigan graduate students and post docs interested in pursuing careers in the consulting industry. To this end, we host different events including weekly case practice meetings, company info sessions, and soon our annual case competition. We also provide resources and personal development services that are necessary for success in consulting interviews.

Sponsorship

The Michigan Graduate Consulting Club welcomes the involvement of consulting firms to promote awareness of the consulting industry and to prepare MGCC members for the field. The MGCC partners with leading firms for workshops, networking, informational panels, and case competitions. Our weekly case prep workshops are highly attended, and we have the most diverse membership base on the University of Michigan campus, with members from all 19 colleges and schools at the university. If you are interested in becoming an MGCC corporate sponsor, or partnering with our organization for an event, please contact us for more information.
