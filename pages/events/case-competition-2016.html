2016 MGCC Case Competition

The 2nd Annual Midwest Business Case Competition

Registration to participate in our 2nd Annual Midwest Business Case Competition is now open. Please note that the deadline to sign up is February 17th. Prizes will be awarded to the first and second teams. Details on prizes, as well as the date and location for the final round presentation will be disclosed soon.

Registration

http://goo.gl/forms/XKt1LC1OBO
The competition is open to advanced degree candidates (PhDs, JDs, MDs and Post-Doc’s) and Master’s students**. In accordance with the purpose and mission of MGCC the competition is NOT open to: MBA, Undergraduates.
** Master’s students need to have at-least one advance degree candidate in their team, to be eligible for prizes.

Team Composition requirements

Teams must have four members with at least one member from a different school or discipline from the other three.

If you do not have a team MGCC will help facilitate one for you be sure to select the appropriate option in the entry form

Tentative timeline

  • February 17th
  • – Last day to sign-up
  • February 19th
  • – Case and guidelines released to teams
  • March 11th
  • – Case slides/solutions due
  • March 14th
  • – Semi finalists announced
  • March 18th (tentative)
  • – Semi Finals (morning)
  • – Lunch with finalists and judges
  • – Final formal presentation (afternoon)
  • – Social open to all participants and MGCC members

Inquiry
