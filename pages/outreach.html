Outreach

The Michigan Graduate Consulting Club welcomes the involvement of consulting firms to promote awareness of the consulting industry and to prepare MGCC members for the field. The MGCC partners with leading firms for workshops, networking, informational panels, and case competitions. Our weekly case prep workshops are highly attended, and we have the most diverse membership base on the University of Michigan campus, with members from all 19 colleges and schools at the university.

Sponsorship

If you are interested in becoming an MGCC corporate sponsor, or partnering with our organization for an event, please contact us for more information.

Recruiters

If you are planning corporate events like info sessions on our campus, please inform us in advance so we can notify our members.
